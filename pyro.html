Pyro

Pyro is one of the 7 elements presetned within the world of Teyvat. It's an alternative name for "Fire", so all the characters that wield the Pyro element can manipulate fire in some sort of way.

With its unique properties, when in tandem with other elements it can cause various "Elemental Reactions" that'll aid your characters.

These reactions include;
Elemental Reaction Effect
Pyro + Electro Causes the enemy to explode, dealing AoE damage.
Overload
Pyro + Anemo Reaction deals extra elemental damage of the element that was applied (in this instance, Pyro)
Swirl
Pyro + Hydro If Pyro comes into contact with an enemy that has been applied with hydro, deal 1.5x Pyro damage
Vaporize
Pyro + Cryo If Pyro comes into contact with an enemy that has been applied with cryo, deal 2x Pyro damage
Melt
